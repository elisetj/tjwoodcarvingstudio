Experience


With over 40 years of experience, Doug Trail-Johnson thoughtfully creates the finest in handcarved items and furnishings for churches, homes and businesses. Doug's B.A. in History from Bethel College has driven his interest in understanding the many intricate woodworking styles and historical significance of the craft. He apprenticed for thirteen years under a Greek Orthodox woodcarver, and was the co-founder of a contracting company. All work is custom-designed to meet your unique needs with a commitment to excellent customer service.
